Taste Journal Support

Quick help for logging, saving and finding your recipes.

Privacy

Account & Login

Can’t log in? Make sure your email and password match what you registered with. Still stuck? Use Contact Us and include your email.

Photos not showing

Dish photos are saved locally on your device. If you reinstall the app, old images may be removed to save space or due to OS clearing.

Shake to random dish

Shake your phone to trigger the cooking animation and jump to a random saved dish. If nothing happens, make sure you have at least 1 dish saved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All recipes are saved locally on your phone (not online). We prioritize your privacy and do not upload your content.

Can I recover deleted dishes?

No — deleting a dish removes it completely from the device database. Please be careful when removing entries.

Do you collect personal data?

No online tracking. Only your device storage is used for app functionality.

Why do I see the login screen again?

If you logged out manually or cleared app data via settings, you’ll need to log in again to access the interface.

Can I export my recipes?

Direct export is currently in development and planned for future updates. Stay tuned!
